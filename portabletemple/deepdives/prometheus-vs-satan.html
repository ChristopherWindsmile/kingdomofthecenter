Prometheus vs. Satan

The two best-known "rebel against the highest power" figures in the Western imagination, set side by side. Same basic shape, different verdict.

PrometheusSatan
Rebels against Zeus by giving fire to humanity Rebels against God by leading a rebellion in heaven
Punished for rebellion — chained to a rock Punished for rebellion — cast down
Suffers for mankind Suffers for himself / his own ambition

The shape is the same — defiance of the ruling order, punishment by exile or binding — but the motive assigned to each figure is what an era decides to make of "rebellion." One is read as sacrifice, the other as pride. Neither reading is forced by the bare facts of the story.
