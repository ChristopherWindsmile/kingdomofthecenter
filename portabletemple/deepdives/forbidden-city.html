The Forbidden City

  • Structure: This is the ultimate Architecture of Enclosure (Stage 5), but of a very specific kind.

  • Geometry: It is a vast, perfect rectangle aligned with the cardinal directions. It is not a cube reaching for heaven, but a cosmic diagram laid flat on the earth. It is the world-square, the realm of "All Under Heaven" (Tianxia) in microcosm.

  • "Perpetual Hearth": The "fire" here is the Emperor's person and the dynastic lineage. The Forbidden City encloses and protects the Son of Heaven, the human pivot of the cosmos. The three main halls on the central axis are the heart of this:

    • Hall of Supreme Harmony (Taihe Dian): The "apex" of the human world, where the Emperor's earthly authority was enacted. It is the symbolic summit of the human-built order.

    • Its function is not to send gifts to a distant god, but to radiate harmonious order outward from the cosmic center. The "fire" is imperial authority, and the Forbidden City is its perfect, bounded container—the Enclosure of the Cosmic-Political Center.
